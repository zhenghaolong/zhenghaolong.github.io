About Me

I am a Master's student at Shandong Normal University, working on multimodal sentiment analysis. My research focuses on understanding human emotions through the fusion of multiple modalities, including text, image, and audio.

Prior to my graduate studies, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics from Changchun University of Technology, where I developed a solid foundation in mathematical modeling and numerical computation.

I am broadly interested in natural language processing, affective computing, and multimodal learning, with the goal of building AI systems that can better understand human communication.
