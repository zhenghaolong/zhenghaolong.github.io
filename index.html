郑豪龙 Haolong Zheng

山东师范大学硕士研究生，研究方向为多模态情感分析。主要从事自然语言处理、情感计算与多模态学习研究，聚焦于文本、图像、音频等多种模态信息的融合与情感理解。

本科毕业于长春工业大学计算数学专业，系统学习了数学分析、数值计算、线性代数与概率统计，具备扎实的数学与计算基础，为深度学习与自然语言处理研究提供了坚实支撑。

研究兴趣涵盖多模态情感分析、自然语言处理、情感计算与深度学习，致力于构建能够更好理解人类情感与语言的智能系统。

I am a Master's student at Shandong Normal University, working on Multimodal Sentiment Analysis. My research focuses on natural language processing, affective computing, and multimodal learning, with an emphasis on fusing information from text, image, and audio modalities to understand human emotions.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics from Changchun University of Technology, where I built a solid foundation in mathematical analysis, numerical computation, linear algebra, and probability theory — providing strong support for my current research in deep learning and NLP.

My research interests include multimodal sentiment analysis, natural language processing, affective computing, and deep learning. I am committed to building intelligent systems that better understand human emotion and language.
